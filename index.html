Our Founder: Jeff White

Born and raised in Barrie, Jeff White founded Protoss Electrical in 2019 after honing his craft at the University of Ottawa and trade school in Midland. With a background at O’Connor Electric, Jeff launched Protoss to bridge the gap between technical mastery and personal service.

His vision was simple: a company where quality work and competitive prices are the baseline, not the exception.

The Protoss Difference

We believe that when our employees do well, our customers do too. Every member of the Protoss team holds a sizable minority share of the company’s net profits. This model fosters a culture of ownership, loyalty, and a personal investment in every wire we pull.
